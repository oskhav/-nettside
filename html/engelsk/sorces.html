Wildlife

Vancouver is rich in wildlife due to its unique combination of coastal, forest and mountain ecosystem. In the surrounding mountains, black bears are common. They are rarely seen in the city. Vancouver is a stop over for many migratory birds. Such as sandpipers, warblers and ducks. Many of the citys streams and rivers are grounds for salmon. Vancouvers rich biodiversity makes it an exiting place for nature lovers.
